About

I am late to this party... I've only recently discovered my passion for computing and now I'm playing catch up in a serious way. I spent the beginning of adulthood climbing, skydiving and exploring the arctic without a care in the world. But now I am on a mission to learn this new realm as thoroughly as possible.

I am at the University of Brighton studying Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence. These are the early days and I'm hungry for knowledge! This is my Journal documenting my progress with web development.
